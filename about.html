Our Journey

Fifteen Years of Excellence

From a two-person startup to one of the UK's most respected cybersecurity consultancies.

2009

Founded in London

Marcus Webb and Dr. Aisha Okonkwo establish Sentinel Shield from a Shoreditch office, initially focused on financial services penetration testing. First client: a mid-tier investment bank seeking CBEST assessment.

2012

CREST Accreditation Achieved

Sentinel Shield receives CREST accreditation, establishing professional credentials that open doors to Crown Commercial Service framework contracts. Team grows to 18 consultants.

2014

ISO 27001 Certification & SOC Launch

Achieve ISO 27001:2013 certification. Launch the first version of our Security Operations Centre, providing 24/7 managed detection and response to NHS and public sector clients.

2017

International Expansion

Open offices in Dublin, Frankfurt and Singapore. Sentinel Shield becomes the cybersecurity partner of record for three FTSE 50 organisations. Team surpasses 50 consultants.

2020

Pandemic Response & Cloud Security Launch

Rapid response to COVID-driven remote working vulnerabilities helps 200+ clients secure distributed workforces. Launch dedicated Cloud Security Assessment practice covering AWS, Azure and GCP environments.

2023

AI Threat Intelligence Platform

Launch Shield Intelligence: a proprietary threat intelligence platform integrating machine learning to predict emerging attack vectors. Awarded SC Media's 'Best Security Company of the Year'.

2024

Today & Beyond

85 consultants. 500+ active clients. 35 countries. Continuing to push the boundaries of what enterprise cybersecurity means in an AI-augmented threat landscape.
