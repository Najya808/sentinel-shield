Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Straightforward answers to questions we hear from security leaders every day.

How long does a penetration test typically take?
Scope and complexity determine the timeline. A web application test for a mid-size platform typically runs 5–10 business days. An enterprise infrastructure engagement covering internal and external networks can take 3–6 weeks. We always agree a clear scope and timeline before commencing — you'll never receive unexpected invoices or schedule overruns.
What happens if a critical vulnerability is found during testing?
We operate a responsible disclosure process. Any critical finding — one that could result in immediate data loss, system compromise or reputational damage — is reported to you verbally within 4 hours of discovery, regardless of where we are in the engagement. You'll receive clear mitigation guidance immediately, with a written technical advisory to follow.
Do you work with small businesses as well as enterprises?
Absolutely. While we work with FTSE 100 companies and government agencies, we also serve growing SMBs who understand that cyber risk doesn't discriminate by company size. We offer a modular engagement model that lets smaller organisations access professional-grade security services at a proportionate investment level.
Can you help us achieve ISO 27001 certification?
Yes. Our compliance consultants have supported over 80 organisations through successful ISO 27001 certification. We provide full gap analysis, Information Security Management System (ISMS) design and documentation, staff training, internal auditing and accompany your team through the formal Stage 1 and Stage 2 certification audits.
How is our data and confidential information protected during an engagement?
We take data handling as seriously as we take security. Before any engagement, we sign a mutual NDA and data processing agreement. All data collected during testing is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256. Testing artefacts are securely destroyed within 30 days of report delivery. Our internal processes are ISO 27001 certified — we hold ourselves to the same standards we advise clients to adopt.
