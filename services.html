Offensive Security

Penetration Testing

Our CREST-certified red team simulates sophisticated, targeted attacks against your networks, applications and people to identify the vulnerabilities that matter most — before a real adversary does.

  • External Network Testing
    Attack simulation from the perspective of an external adversary targeting your internet-facing systems, services and perimeter.
  • Web Application Testing
    Manual and automated testing against OWASP Top 10, business logic flaws, authentication weaknesses and API vulnerabilities.
  • Mobile Application Testing
    Comprehensive iOS and Android security assessments covering data storage, communication, authentication and code quality.
  • Cloud Configuration Review
    AWS, Azure and GCP misconfiguration assessment with privilege escalation path analysis and IAM policy review.
  • Social Engineering
    Targeted phishing, vishing and physical entry simulation to test your human firewall under realistic threat conditions.

Know Your Risk

Risk & Vulnerability Assessment

Before you can reduce risk, you must understand it precisely. Our structured assessment methodology maps your entire attack surface, scores every vulnerability by exploitability and business impact, and delivers a prioritised remediation roadmap your team can act on immediately.

  • Asset Discovery & Inventory
    Complete enumeration of your digital estate — known and shadow IT — using active scanning and passive monitoring techniques.
  • CVSS-Based Vulnerability Scoring
    Every finding scored using the CVSS 3.1 framework, contextualised by your specific business environment and threat model.
  • Executive & Technical Reporting
    Board-ready risk summary with business impact language, plus full technical appendix for your security and IT operations teams.
  • Prioritised Remediation Roadmap
    Findings sequenced by risk reduction value, effort and cost — so your team invests resource where it has the greatest impact.

Incident Response

A confirmed breach is a race against time. Every hour of uncontained attacker access increases data loss, regulatory exposure and reputational damage. Our incident response retainer guarantees a qualified senior analyst begins triage within 60 minutes of your call.

  • 1-Hour Response SLA
    IR retainer clients receive guaranteed 60-minute response with on-site deployment within 4 hours to any UK mainland location.
  • Containment & Eradication
    Immediate isolation of affected systems, attacker eviction, backdoor removal and verification of clean environment restoration.
  • Digital Forensics
    Court-admissible forensic imaging and analysis to establish the full attack timeline, scope of compromise and data exfiltration.
  • Regulatory Notification Support
    Expert guidance through ICO, FCA, PRA and sector-specific breach notification obligations with template documentation.

Compliance Consulting

  • ISO 27001:2022 Certification
    Full implementation programme from gap analysis through ISMS design to successful Stage 1 and Stage 2 certification audit support.
