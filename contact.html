Before You Contact Us

Common Questions

How quickly will someone respond to my enquiry?
We respond to all general enquiries within 4 business hours. Urgent project requests typically receive a detailed response within the same business day. For active incidents, our emergency line connects you directly to an on-call incident commander within minutes.
Is the initial consultation really free?
Yes, completely. Our initial consultations are 30-minute calls with a senior practitioner — not a sales representative. We'll discuss your current situation honestly and tell you whether we can add value. There's no obligation to proceed and no hard-sell.
Do you work with organisations outside the UK?
Yes. While our headquarters and primary team are in London, we have offices in Dublin, Frankfurt and Singapore, and have delivered engagements in over 35 countries. Remote assessments are available for all services, and on-site work can be arranged globally.
What information should I have ready before calling?
For a project enquiry, it helps to know roughly how many systems, applications or people are in scope, your preferred timeline, and any specific compliance requirements you're working towards. For an active incident, just call — our team will gather what they need as they begin the triage process.
